Established in 2009, the Fitness Studio LLC is located at 400 Warwick Ave, Warwick, RI. We are an exercise studio specializing in group fitness, while providing personalized, individual attention to our members. We also provide personal training services and strive to motivate, educate and make Fitness Fun!

Join us today and reach your goals! Get Fit! Have Fun! The First class is Free!!!!

The Fitness Studio LLC provides a unique atmosphere in which to work out:

  • a large, 2200 sq. ft. suspended hardwood floor
  • Air Conditioned Aerobics Room
  • Ample on-site parking
  • Conveniently located in Warwick
  • Highly Qualified, Certified Instructors
