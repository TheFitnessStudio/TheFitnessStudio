Established in 2009, the Fitness Studio LLC is Rhode Island's popular exercise studio specializing in group fitness, while providing personalized, individual attention to its members.

The Fitness Studio LLC provides a unique atmosphere in which to work out: -

  • a large, 2200 sq. ft. suspended hardwood floor
  • Air Conditioned Aerobics Room
  • Ample on-site parking
  • Conveniently located in Warwick
  • Highly Qualified, Certified Instructors
